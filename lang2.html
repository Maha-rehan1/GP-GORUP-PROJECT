About

Dr. Fabeha Syed She is a Multimedia Producer for a podcast named ''Quint''. From her academic background, she was a student pursuing MA multimedia Journalism at the university of WestMinister, London. Her research profile includes her skills in Medieval literature, Comparative Literature, Literature studies and Literary criticism. Her academic background suggests she has expertise in language, especially in urdu and literature.

In her podcast episode, where she talks about brainrot as ''Zavaal'' which refers to decline/ downfall of modern malaise, she discusses brainrot being the Oxford word of the year, which describes mental burnout and consumption of low-quality content online because of endless scrolling, connecting it to the urdu word, ''Zavaal'' (zehni zavaal). The podcast explores the cultural and personal effects of brainrot by using imagery of setting suns and philosophical insights on life's inevitable cycles.

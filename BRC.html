Tung Tung Tung Sahur is an Indonesian brainrot character made by Noxa, an Indonesian tiktoker who created several different Italian brainrot characters alongside AI videos. Tung Tung Tung Sahur is a log who wields a baseball bat as a weapon who was pubished on the 1st of March, 2025. The word “Tung” represents the beat of a kentongan, which is a traditional Indonesian communication tool made from hollowed-out bamboo or wood, struck with a mallet to produce loud sounds for local alerts. And the word “Sahur” also written as Sahoor is an islamic tradition in which Muslims wake up at dawn before sun rises on the days of Ramadan, to prepare for the fast. So the meaning behind this character was to wake up the muslims at a proper time so they can carry out sahur. It’s an abnormal creature that appears during Sahur. It’s said that if someone is called for sahur three times and does not answer, then this creature will come to your house.

Abilities : Superhuman Physical Characteristics Inorganic Physiology (Tung Tung Tung Sahur is an "anomaly" that resembles a humanoid night patrol drum) Teleportation, possibly Resurrection (Low-Godly; During his fight with Brr Brr Patapim, he was able to escape his trap, although, it's debatable if he came back with an unknown teleportation ability, or if he came back to life) Telekinesis (Able to telekinetically pull his own bat) Body Control and Transformation (Has been shown to be able to shapeshift his body, turning even bigger in his Second Form)

Translation : Tung tung tung tung tung tung tung tung tung sahur. A terrifying anomaly that only comes out at Sahoor. It is said that if someone is called for Sahoor three times and does not answer, then this creature comes to your house. Ooh, so scary! This tung tung usually makes a sound like a gong hitting a baton; Tung tung tung tung. Share it with your friends who find it difficult to wake up for Sahoor.

Since then, the Tung Tung Tung Sahur trend has absolutely exploded across TikTok. Creators everywhere are putting their own spin on it: recreating the sound, remixing the audio, and animating a host of AI characters using the iconic chant. The Tung Tung Tung Sahur filter has only added to the meme magic, making participation in the trend a creative badge of honor on the platform. People have also made stories out of this character showing how this has become a huge part of our writings.

Dr. Ananya Sharma

About

Dr. Ananya Sharma is a child psychologist with over 15 years of experience in cognitive and emotional development. She specializes in the effects of digital media on children and adolescents.

View on Brainrot

According to Dr. Sharma, the rise of short-form content and continuous dopamine hits from social media are rewiring young brains. This overstimulation leads to:

  • Shortened attention spans
  • Difficulty retaining long-form information
  • Reduced critical thinking
  • Increased anxiety and restlessness
